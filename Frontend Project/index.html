Aarogya Hospital Most Innovative Multispeciality Hospital In Delhi & Ghaziabad

Aarogya Hospital is a compact, 100 bedded Multi-speciality Hospital & IVF Centre in Vaishali, Ghaziabad and 35 Multi-speciality Hospital bedded in Chitra Vihar, Delhi founded by Padamshree Awardee Late Dr. K.P.S Varma an eminent internationally acclaimed pediatrician with over 55 years of clinical knowledge who was honorary physician to the president of India for over 40 years. Started in 2003, the hospital has ensured Homely Care, comfort, Healing through compassion and best of clinical expertise to its patients from the last 19 years. Accredited with the prestigious NABH, Aarogya Hospital is known for its quality, commitment and state of the art World class facilities at affordable cost for a common man to ensure equity in healthcare industry throughout Delhi-NCR. We at Aarogya Hospital are earnestly and continuously striving our best to scale newer heights in the pursuit of excellence, with an earnest and dedicated mind to uphold the noble profession and to flourish, year after year. We are committed to make the community avail the benefits of the most modern and technological developments in the field of medical sciences.We offer best in class services in super-specialties like Gastro-enterology, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Reproductive medicines, Orthopedics, Paediatrics, Cardiology, Neurosurgery, Neurology, Medicines, General Surgery, Dentistry, Dietetics and Physiotherapy to name a few and is empanelled with All TPAs, GIPSA Preferred Provider Network and all major corporates.
